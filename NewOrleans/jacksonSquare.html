Jackson Square


Jackson Square is a historic park located in the heart of the French Quarter in New Orleans, Louisiana. It is named after Andrew Jackson, the seventh President of the United States and hero of the Battle of New Orleans. The square is surrounded by iconic landmarks including St. Louis Cathedral, the Cabildo, and the Presbytere. Jackson Square is a popular gathering place for locals and visitors alike, and is often filled with street performers, artists, and musicians. It is also a popular spot for events and festivals throughout the year, including the famous New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival. The park's picturesque setting and historical significance make it a must-visit destination in New Orleans.
