Chalmette Battlefield


The Chalmette Battlefield is a historic site located in Chalmette, Louisiana, just outside of New Orleans. It was the site of the famous Battle of New Orleans, which took place on January 8, 1815, during the War of 1812 between the United States and Great Britain. The battle was a significant victory for the United States, as it halted a British invasion force and boosted American morale. Today, the Chalmette Battlefield is a national historic site operated by the National Park Service. Visitors can explore the battlefield, visit the visitor center and museum, and learn about the history of the battle and its significance in American history.
